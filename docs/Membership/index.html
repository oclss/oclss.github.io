    Membership

    There are two types of membership: full members and associate members.

    Full members are entitled to free food and drinks offered at the monthly seminars and free attendance at any ad hoc events.

    Associate members can only attend ad hoc events if there are seats available, and they will have to buy an unsubsidized ticket for each event.

    The monthly seminars and Friday tea breaks are nevertheless open to the public.

    Registration fee

    There are different rates for full membership, depending on when you register (and pay the fee) and if you are a student:

    Early bird rate (before 31 July 2014):
    Staff £15
    Student £5
    After 31 July 2014
    Staff £20
    Student £10

    To encourage people to join our society, there is no fee for registering as associate members.
