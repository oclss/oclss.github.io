Oxford Life Science Association

The aims of this society are:

  • To bring together researchers with different areas of expertise in life sciences in Oxford
  • To provide a platform for life scientists in Oxford to meet, exchange ideas and establish research collaborations

We welcome anyone working in life sciences in Oxford to join us!
