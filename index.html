    About us

    Several members of the committee helped to organize the very successful 19th Annual Meeting of the Chinese Life Scientists Society in the UK which took place in Oxford in 2013 (for details, please click here). They believed that a permanent platform was needed for Chinese life scientists in Oxford to meet regularly and exchange ideas, and therefore established this society.

    The aims of this society are:

    • To bring together researchers with different areas of expertise in life sciences in Oxford
    • To provide a platform for Chinese life scientists in Oxford to meet, exchange ideas and establish research collaborations

    We welcome anyone working in life sciences in Oxford to join us! Hello World
